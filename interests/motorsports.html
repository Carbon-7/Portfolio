Step 1 · Spark

Lights out, heart up

A random Sunday broadcast turned ritual. I came for the speed; I stayed for the bravery in the braking zone and the calm on the radio.

Step 2 · Engineering

Math at 300 km/h

Tire life versus pace, fuel windows, aero balance—racecraft is applied engineering. It taught me to love problems that have moving answers.

Step 3 · Fandom

Max and Porsche

In F1 I follow Max Verstappen—the relentlessness and clarity. In endurance I back Porsche—the patience to win at sunrise after a night of chaos.

Step 4 · Motivation

Turn another lap

On low days, motorsport reminds me: pace comes from consistency. You don’t quit on worn tires—you adapt, breathe, and find grip anyway.
